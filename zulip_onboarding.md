# New to Globias?

Welcome to the GloBIAS community chat space! If you are new, here are a few things to get you started:

## 1. Introduce yourself in the #**Hello, my name is>** chat

You can write about what you do, where you are based, your favourite image analysis tools, your scientific background, your hobbies and anything else you would like others to know.

If you’re unsure about what to write find some inspiration in the previous posts and reuse them as a template 😊

We also recommend [adding a profile picture](https://zulip.com/help/change-your-profile-picture)!
